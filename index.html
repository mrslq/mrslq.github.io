About Alexander Quinn

Quinny's love of training has grown from a very young age. Studying exercise and sports science at Exeter University helped him to further explore this passion and led him to Body Development. His first few months as a P.T in the industry saw him assisting and spotting Charles Poliquin, something not many P.Ts can say! His ongoing personal development here has included assisting Tom on his hugely successful and effective Mentorship program as well as representing AOM at Body Power and Transform Fitness in Dubai and attending Hulk Enterprises Hypertrophy training days.

One of his particular passions within the industry is the bodyweight and gymnastic style of training. Experiencing Ido Portals Movement X workshop greatly helped improve his knowledge and drive to succeed in this area, both for himself and his clients.

Relevant Qualifications :

  • BD/AOM Mentorship
  • Bsc Exercise and Sport Science, University of Exeter
  • REPS Level 3 Personal Training

    Coach

    Coach Quinny works out of Body Development in Bath. He is there to help you with all your goals. Whether it’s fat loss, performance, health or muscle mass. He will guide and motivate you to achieve your best!

    Coach Quinny offers one to one personal training sessions and group classes for all ages and abilities.

    Movement Method

    Quinny co-founded Movement Method with Jonathan Last in 2016. Together they run workshops and training weekends. They also offer online training based around hand balancing, movement and calisthenics.
